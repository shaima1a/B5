# Topic Modeling

BERTopic: Build a modern 6-step topic-modeling pipeline with BERTopic to gain insights from thousands of unstructured data points, understanding sentiment, common issues, trends, categories, inherent hierarchy, and enable semantic search, for recommendation and other use cases.

Sentence Trasnformer: https://www.sbert.net/docs/sentence_transformer/usage/usage.html


| **Use Case**               | **Problem Solved**                                                                                                                              |
| -------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Customer Feedback**      | Automatically categorizing thousands of reviews or support tickets to identify the most common pain points without manual reading.              |
| **Content Recommendation** | Recommending articles or products based on the underlying themes of what a user has previously engaged with, rather than just keyword matching. |
| **Regulatory Compliance**  | Scanning vast archives of internal communications or legal documents to flag specific "topics" related to risk or non-compliance.               |
| **Trend Analysis**         | Monitoring social media or news over time to see which topics are gaining "velocity" and which are fading.                                      |
| **Academic Research**      | Quickly mapping out the landscape of a new field by analyzing the themes across thousands of published abstracts.                               |
